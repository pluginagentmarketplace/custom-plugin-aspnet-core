## Cybersecurity Essentials

### Key Areas
1. **Network Security:** Firewalls, VPNs, intrusion detection
2. **Application Security:** Input validation, SQL injection prevention
3. **Cryptography:** Encryption, hashing, digital signatures
4. **Access Control:** Authentication, authorization, RBAC
5. **Incident Response:** Detection, containment, recovery

### Tools
- Burp Suite (penetration testing)
- Nmap (network scanning)
- Wireshark (packet analysis)
- OWASP Top 10 knowledge

### Certifications
- CompTIA Security+
- Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)
- OSCP (Offensive Security)
